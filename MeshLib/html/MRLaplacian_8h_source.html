21// Laplacian to smoothly deform a region preserving mesh fine details.
22// How to use:
23// 1. Initialize Laplacian for the region being deformed, here region properties are remembered.
24// 2. Change positions of some vertices within the region and call fixVertex for them.
25// 3. Optionally call updateSolver()
26// 4. Call apply() to change the remaining vertices within the region
27// Then steps 1-4 or 2-4 can be repeated.
