Detailed Description

This metric minimizes summary projection of new edges to plane normal, (try do produce edges parallel to plane)

getTriangleMetric - ac projection to normal getEdgeMetric - -ab projection to normal (it is needed to count last edge only once, as far as edge metric is called only for edge that connects parts of triangulation)
