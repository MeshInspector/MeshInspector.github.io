Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename I>
class MR::Buffer< T, I >

std::vector<T>-like container that is 1) resized without initialization of its elements, 2) much simplified: no push_back and many other methods

Template Parameters
Ttype of stored elements
Itype of index (shall be convertible to size_t)
