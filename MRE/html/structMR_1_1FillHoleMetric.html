Detailed Description

Provides interface for controlling fillHole and buildCylinderBetweenTwoHoles triangulation
.

This is abstract struct used as optimization metric of fillHole and buildCylinderBetweenTwoHoles functions
getTriangleMetric is called for each new triangle candidate and its two known neighbors
getEdgeMetric is called only once when two part of triangulation are merging (edge between two last triangles)
